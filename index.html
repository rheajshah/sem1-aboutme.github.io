About

Hello and welcome to my website!

I'm Rhea Shah, 16 years old, and a current junior in high school; owner of Students for Students, a premier one-on-one tutoring service; and a Science Olympaid captain for American High School. My main interests lie in STEM focused competition and organizing community events for students interested in STEM, as well as Bollywood dance, graphic design, and baking. I am an aspiring computer science major and have cultivated my coding talents through programs at Berkeley and creating websites for various organizations.

The aim of this website is to take you all along through my journey in high school — which classes I have taken / am currently involved in, my extra curricular activites, both academic and creative, and also introduce you to my friends and family!
